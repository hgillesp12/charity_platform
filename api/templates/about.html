Our vision

At the heart of [CollaborAid] is the belief that collaboration is the key to creating a more effective and impactful humanitarian ecosystem. The current competitive model often discourages coordination and information sharing, resulting in inefficiencies. Our platform is designed to provide a space for charities to seamlessly collaborate and share information to eliminate duplications and bridge gaps in humanitarian action.

Key Features

  • Schedule: Add and manage weekly events through our scheduling system.
  • Bulletin Board: Stay informed with the latest updates from charities across the city.
  • Interactive Map: Explore collaboration possibilities in your area!
